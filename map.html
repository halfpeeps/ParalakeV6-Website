Green Areas

In green areas you can mug other players and be completely out of view from the public.

Yellow Areas

In yellow areas you should take extra care to make sure you are out of view of the public. This may mean forcing the player you are attempting to mug into a more hidden part of the area.

Unmarked Areas

You may not mug other players in unmarked areas.

Exceptions

- All private property is a muggable location. (You should still take care not to be in view of those outside)

- All Drug Dealer locations are muggable areas.
